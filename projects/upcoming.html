Title launching soon - Subsonic mobile client

Coming in the future to iOS. I am designing and developing an app to make use of Subsonic from your mobile phone. I will be aiming to create an app as feature rich as the default music app with all the internet capabilities of services like Spotify, giving users complete control over their music collection while on the go and at home.

It will be design and UX focused and include help and support not only on the app but also to those who want to set up a service such as Subsonic for their own music collection, freeing them from the cost of media streaming services. More details will follow soon, app name to be released when domain is secured.
