Its Matt Allen

I’m also known as sdmix around the web, may sound a bit like a DJ, but I wouldn’t try and hire me to play at your next club night, instead I’m a creative. I focus on interactivity, photography and film. Creating awesome user interfaces and journeys through products that customers love to use.

Making it up as I go along.

I like to dive into anything creative. The things I’ve done in my life shows that I’m not afraid to dive into the unknown, to learn by doing and to get the job done. I don’t shy away from a challenge; in fact I thrive on them. I’m at my best when I’m around creative people, doing what they love to do. As an avid photographer, I’ve always shown my life through pictures; this year I hope to transfer the skills I’ve accumulated to the medium of film.

They say:

“Find a job doing what you love, and you’ll never work a day in your life.” — Confucius

This is what I do, and this is what I love.

What I do, what I’ve done

I currently work as a Creative Designer at BSkyB working within Sky Betting & Gaming. I have a Bachelor of Arts (BAHons) in Interactive Design from the awesome University of Lincoln.

In the past, I’ve done many freelance photography shoots, focusing mainly on portraits/group shots, these skills then lead me to cinematography and diving into the world of video. I get involved activily online and you can find my work from around the web using the links below.
